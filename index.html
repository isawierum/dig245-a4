BACKGROUND

In a world evolving to use zoom for everything from school to trivia night, we decided it was important to see how user-friendly their website is. We conducted a usability test on a fellow classmate to learn more.

TASKS

Log into zoom

Our subject was already logged in, but she was able to repeat the process easily.

Allow share screen

The longest task (~30 s), our subject only found the correct setting with command+f because the page had too much information.

Enable waiting room

Now familiar with the difficulty of finding a specific setting, the user automatically used command+f to complete the task.

Disable password for meeting room

Again, our subject used command+f to locate the setting in question.

Allow recording

The subject located the recordings tab from memory, and used command+f once again.
